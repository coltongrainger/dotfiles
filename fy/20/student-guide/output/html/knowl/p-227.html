Write the equation in the slope-intercept form to find the intercept:

\begin{align*} x + 3y\amp= 6\amp\amp\text{Subtract \(x\) from both sides}\\ \amp\amp\amp\text{Divide both sides by \(3\)}\\ y\amp= -\frac{1}{3}x+2\amp\amp\text{Slope-intercept form} \end{align*}

We see the \(y\)-intercept is \(2.\checkmark\)
