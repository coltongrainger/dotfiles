WARNING.

The Power of a Product Rule only applies to products. It does not apply to sums for any exponent not equal to \(1\text{.}\) For example,

\begin{align*} (1+2)^2 = 3^2 = 9 \amp\amp \text{But,}\\ 1^2+2^2 = 1+4 = 5 \amp\amp \text{So, }\\ (1+2)^2 \neq 1^2+2^2 \amp\amp \text{The rule does not hold for a sum.} \end{align*}
\begin{align*} (a+b)^m\ne a^m+b^m \amp\amp\amp \text{These are NOT equal if \(m\ne 1\), beware of this error!} \end{align*}
