To consider these two equations “simultaneously” is to ask the question:

\begin{gather*} \text{What }(x, y)\text{ pairs make both equations true?} \end{gather*}

To express the same question in terms of functions: What values can you hand the functions \(2\sqrt{x}\) and \(x + \frac{1}{2}\) that will make these two functions produce the same answer?
